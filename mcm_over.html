Mathematical Contest in Modeling

The MCM is an international contest for college undergraduates. It challenges teams of students to clarify, analyze, and propose solutions to open-ended problems. The contest attracts diverse students and faculty advisors from over 900 institutions around the world. Each team of up to three people has four days to deliver a 25 page report on an open-ended question that requires the team to develop a mathematical model. I competed in 2019 and 2020, and was lucky enough to win in 2019! This competition has a special place in my heart, so I hope you enjoy reading these!
